# The 1960s Virtual 8-Track Console

**An interactive terminal-based simulation of vintage audio engineering workflows.**

## Overview

This project simulates the high-stakes environment of a 1960s recording studio. As the Lead Sound Engineer, you must manage a limited **8-track tape machine** to record and mix a musical session.

## Mechanics

- **Tracking:** Use Tracks 1–3 for initial instrument recording.
- **Bouncing:** Combine tracks to free up space, but watch your quality!
- **Sonic Degradation:**
  _ 1–2 Bounces: Pristine
      _ 3–4 Bounces: Warm
      _ 5–6 Bounces: Muddy
      _ 7+ Bounces: Unusable
- **Frequency Logic:** Be careful not to stack too many low-end instruments on the same track!
